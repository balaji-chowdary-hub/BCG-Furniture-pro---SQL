# Overview
This project analyzes a simulated BCG client dataset for a furniture retail business (Furniture Pro). Using SQL, the analysis focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability to generate actionable business insights.

# Objectives
Analyze overall sales and revenue performance
Identify top-performing products and categories
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Support business decisions with data-driven insights

# KPIs Analyzed
Total Revenue
Total Orders
Average Order Value
Total Customers
Category-wise and Product-wise Revenue
Regional Sales Performance

# Tools & Technologies
MySQL
SQL (Joins, Aggregations, Subqueries, Filtering)

# Key Insights
Identified high-revenue product categories driving business growth
Highlighted best-selling products contributing the largest revenue share
Discovered customer purchase patterns and spending behavior
Regional analysis revealed strong and weak market areas
Insights support inventory optimization and revenue strategy

Conclusion
This SQL-based analysis provides a structured understanding of business performance and customer trends, enabling data-driven decision-making similar to real-world consulting projects.
